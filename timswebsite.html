DETAILS

This is a new model of website of our group project TIMS some are same ideas but with new approach and added features. I used PHP, HTML, Js and CSS, Bootstrap for the design and phpMyAdmin for the database.

Functions/Features

A. The user must first log in to the system in order to access his or her account.
B. Without logging in or registering, interested individuals can browse the available courses and additional information about Provincial Training Center and TESDA.
C. Qualified learners will be alerted when new slots and open courses become available, so they can stay up to date directly from the LGU office.
D. The data in the system will be updated automatically.
E. The system will provide current information regarding the slot availability as well as open courses for new learners.
