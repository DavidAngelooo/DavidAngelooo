Program Description

The main purpose of the online retail application project is to display data or information about the products, the storeâ€™s orders from various retailers, and records of registered customers and retailers and it will also display records of products purchased. The website is a clothing store called RAJA. The store's name is derived from the first letters of each group member's name. The navigation bar on the home page includes the following items: home, products, account record, order record, and a shopping bag icon.
The featured and latest products, as well as client testimonials, are displayed on the home page. On the product page, you'll find a list of all available products as well as information about each one, such as the title, price, rating, and retailer. Once you've clicked on a product, you'll be sent to a more full description that includes many photographs of the product from various angles, the retailer, and stock availability. The list of registered costumers and retailers can be found on the account record. The order record will show a list of what the website purchased from various retailers. The things that the customer ordered from the website will be displayed on the shopping bag icon. The list will include product images, prices, quantities, subtotals, customer IDs, shipping fees, discounts, totals, and payment methods.
